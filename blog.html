The Science Behind Trichoderma Viride: Nature's Shield for Crop Protection

Trichoderma viride is a beneficial soil fungus revolutionizing sustainable agriculture through natural crop protection. This microscopic organism protects plants from pathogenic fungi while enhancing growth and soil health through multiple mechanisms: mycoparasitism, antibiosis, competition, and plant growth promotion. Its applications include seed treatment, soil application, and foliar spray, offering benefits like disease prevention, improved root development, and increased crop yields. Unlike chemical pesticides, Trichoderma viride maintains soil biodiversity and supports organic farming practices. Its growing adoption reflects its effectiveness in providing sustainable crop protection while preserving ecological balance in modern agriculture.
